Abandoned Media

A website archiving all sorts of abandoned media from video games, movies, TV shows, music, E-books and audiobooks.

Abandonware refers to software, particularly older video games and applications, no longer supported, sold, or marketed by its original rights holder. While often available on enthusiast websites for download, it usually remains under copyright, occupying a legal gray area. It is commonly used for digital preservation, nostalgia, or running legacy systems.
